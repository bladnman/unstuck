## Understanding

The current auth flow has messy token handling and it's been a frustration for a while. The idea is to rebuild it from scratch with proper OAuth, which would unlock third-party integrations — something the product team has been asking about "forever." But it's recognized as a big lift that can't start until after Q2 planning. There's energy and excitement here but also a realistic sense of timing constraints.
